# Stone & Standard v2.0.3

The gym side finds its voice — sort of.

v2.0.2 wired voice clips into throws celebrations and left lifts
silent on purpose: a coach-style phrase doesn't land the same way
when you're under a bar. v2.0.3 fixes that with the literal sound
of the room — three plates loading onto the bar, a beat to settle,
then the drop.

## What plays when

**Lift PR or Lift Goal achieved** — a four-clip sequence:

```
loading the bar →  loading the bar  →  loading the bar  →  barbell drop
       (1s gap)           (1s gap)            (1.9s settle)
```

About nine seconds of audio that mirrors the moment itself —
plates clicking onto the bar in a rhythm, a beat to settle into
position, then the bar dropping after the rep finishes.

Both Lift PR and Lift Goal cards play the same sequence. They're
both lift milestones; both moments call for the same audio
language.

## What's not changed

- Throws PR / Goal / height-event / Awesome Day voice clips from
  v2.0.2 still play exactly as they did.
- Multi-card queue policy unchanged — only the first card in a save
  fires its audio; middle cards stay silent; Awesome Day always
  plays at the end.
- Sound stays off by default; the toggle inside the celebration
  overlay turns it on.
- Replays via View Celebrations stay silent.

## Migration notes

Open the app. The next time you hit a lift PR or lift Goal with
sound on, you'll hear it.
